High Frequency Inverter for wireless power transfer system

Designed an H-Bridge Inverter capable of output frequencies upto 85Khz for maximum power rating of 500 watts. Used IRF3205 mosfet for the inverter. Simulations conducted Ansys for capacitive and inductive power transfer. ANSYS 2024 R1 and KiCad 7.0 was used in this project.

Additionally I worked on power electronic converters and compensation network on project titled "Design and Optimization of a Lightweight, Aerodynamic, and Misalignment-Tolerant Wireless Charging System for Un-manned Aerial Vehicles."
